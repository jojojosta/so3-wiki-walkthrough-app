ITEMS AND EQUIPMENT

This section details the various items found in Star Ocean: Till the End of Time, including weapons, armor, accessories, consumables, and key items.

Stat Explanations: Attack [ATK], Defense [DEF], Intelligence [INT], Hit Accuracy [HIT], Agility [AGL], Evaluation [EVA], Value [VAL]. Base stats are added to the character's stats.

Obtaining Items: Explains how to obtain the item (Shops, Item Creation, Chests, Drops). Prioritizes initial or stable locations.

Factors: Lists the inherent effects of the item. Notes if factors can be duplicated or erased via Item Creation.

WEAPONS

Playable characters use weapons specific to their fighting style. Fayt uses swords, Cliff and Mirage use gauntlets, etc. The Laser Weapon is usable by all. Experiment and enhance weapons via Item Creation and Synthesis.
